About

My name is Sam and I am a software engineering student passionate about AI, and specficially the ethical and safety guidelines that will be required to build and maintain safe, modern systems.

As mentioned on the home page, this is a blog (WIP) that provides the opportunity for me to discuss ideas in the form of opinion pieces or more in-depth research articles.

Why this site exists

Throughout my time at university, AI has developed faster than the world anticipated; with no signs of slowing down. I believe that with such rapid change, it is imperative to consider how to best create a set of guidelines and laws to safely accomodate for this rampant growth in Australia. Inspiration can be taken from the EU's responses, where they have currently placed a priority on the ethical use of AI, with the flexibility provided to continually develop and improve upon any guidelines currently being developed.

What's not here

There's no newsletter, no comments, no account system, no tracking. I made those choices on purpose, as seen in the privacy page for more details.
